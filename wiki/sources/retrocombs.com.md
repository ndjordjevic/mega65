Steven Combs' (retroCombs) curated "MEGA65 Resource Page" — a maintained quick-reference catalog of "all things MEGA65" — plus the index of his User's Guide series, where he works through the official MEGA65 User's Guide chapter-by-chapter in paired blog posts and YouTube videos. Less technical depth than [[dansanderson.com]], but the best single catalog of download links (essentials, cores, tools) and the only structured video companion to the official manual.

_All claims below are sourced from ../../raw/web/retrocombs.com.md unless otherwise noted._

## What it does

A link hub organized into: User's Guide series, blog posts, videos, his own BASIC 65 programs (Color Palette demo, a C128-Programmer's-Reference submarine-tracking recreation), official information, essentials, cores, technical documents, tools, community, and gear.

## Key features

- **User's Guide series** (retrocombs.com/mega65-ug-1 … -5 published; more planned through PETSCII/screen codes): Introduction, Setup, Getting Started, Configuring, Upgrading — each with a YouTube video and a companion post carrying links and errata.
- **Essentials download list** (all Filehost links): latest `MEGA65.ROM`, SD Card Essentials, SD Card Intro Disk Extras, GEOS 65 beta, Freezer V2 (in development, github.com/M3wP/MEGA65-Freezer).
- **Cores list**: C64 core (IEC, cartridge, REU support), Galaga, Game Boy/GBC, stable Release core, ZX Uno (Spectrum).
- **Tools**: Xemu, M65 Connect (GUI), MEGA65 Tools release package (`m65`, `mega65_ftp` CLI).
- **Gear**: Trenz shop, COMMAND webshop, Hyperkin Trooper joystick, mouSTer USB-mouse adapter and Tank Mouse (1351 substitutes — flagged as "a must for GEOS").

## Architecture and concepts

Pure curation — the page's value is its maintenance promise ("I'll keep the updates coming") and its categorization. Technical documents section mirrors the official book set ([[mega65.org]]) and adds the MEGA65 Style Guide for documentation contributors.

## Main APIs

Key URLs: resource page at `/mega65`, series index at `/mega65-users-guide`, chapter posts at `/mega65-ug-N`, MEGA65 video playlist on the retroCombs YouTube channel.

## When to use

When you need a download link fast (ROM, SD essentials, cores, tools) or want a guided video walkthrough of the official User's Guide instead of reading it cold. Also the most practical accessory-buying guide (mice, joysticks).

## Ecosystem

Links out to every major hub: [[mega65.org]], Filehost, c65gs.blogspot.com (Paul Gardner-Stephen), MEGA museum (m-e-g-a.org), Dan's Digest, Discord, Forum64, Reddit. Note: its "alternative cores" link points at the older sy2002.github.io/m65cores list; the current catalog is cores.mega65.org.
