## Overview

Community roundup: Commodore International revival, COMPUTE!'s Gazette return, VCF West recap, new tutorials from retroCombs and RetroCogs.

## Key Announcements

**Commodore International**: Perifractic (Retro Recipes YouTube) launched Commodore.net with pre-orders for the "Commodore 64 Ultimate" (modern all-in-one system). Dan Sanderson is a volunteer partner alongside "a real who's-who of Commodore veterans."

**COMPUTE!'s Gazette Revival**: Edwin Nagle revived the classic magazine; Sanderson contributes a regular MEGA65 Digest column syndicating feature articles.

## Community Content

- **retroCombs**: completed video series covering all chapters of the MEGA65 User's Guide
- **RetroCogs**: documented advanced VIC-IV techniques (Full Color Mode, Raster Rewrite Buffer)
- **Level Up with Jeffrey**: gameplay videos on compatibility modes and programming

## VCF West (Computer History Museum, Mountain View CA)

- Over 3,500 attendees
- Sanderson gave the keynote (first speaker of the event)
- Met Paul Lassa (original C65 designer) — "the highlight of the event for me"
- Also met Kenneth Dyke (worked on Amiga + MEGA65 debugger)
