## Overview

Results of the MEGA65 Community Survey 2023 (509 responses): user demographics, hardware configurations, software interests, community resource awareness. Also covers v0.96 testing, Ethernet file transfer, and revised User's Guide. Mostly community data; references PAL/NTSC modes, JTAG/UART dev tools, Xemu, and cc65 but no programming detail.
