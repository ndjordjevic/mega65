## Overview

Merchandise + patronage announcement. The **45GS02 Quick Reference** mousepads and posters list every 6502, 65CE02 (4510), and 45GS02 instruction and addressing mode, color-coded by CPU, with status register effects and branch behaviors. Ko-fi introduced as a support platform for the Digest. Minimal technical content (the reference card itself is a useful artifact).
