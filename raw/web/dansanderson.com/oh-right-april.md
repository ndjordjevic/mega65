## Overview

Brief community update (no feature article that month). Two notable releases:

- **Pascal65**: a compiler and IDE for the Pascal language on the MEGA65.
- **llvm-mos SDK header files**: enable C/C++ access to MEGA65 hardware — VIC-IV, SID, CIA, hardware math, and the Hypervisor.
