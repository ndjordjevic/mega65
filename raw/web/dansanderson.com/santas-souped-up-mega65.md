## Overview

December community roundup: new tools and demos rather than continuing the Crossroads disassembly series.

## Notable Releases

**SidPlay65** (by LightTangent): Reimplementation of Compute!'s SIDPlayer music system. Plays single and dual-SID songs; supports PETSCII displays and karaoke lyrics. Disassembled original Compute! SIDPlayer to inform implementation. Thousands of SID songs from C64 era compatible.

**Full-Color Video Demos** (by MirageBD):
- *HyperBallad* (Björk music video) — streams from SD card
- *Duel* (from film *Electric Dreams*) — streams from SD card
- Requires fast microSD: SanDisk Ultra recommended

**COPA65** (by Bananzi): Copy-paste clipboard extension for the built-in screen editor. Select text with keyboard/mouse; Ctrl+C standard clipboard operations.

**Midnight Mega** (by Bruno): SD card file browser; mount disk images; manage files. Beta — requires regular data backups.

**Sea Wolf Disassembly** (by Gurce): Complete disassembly of classic submarine game; modified for 4-player gameplay. Demonstrates reverse-engineering as a teaching tool for C64/MEGA65 techniques.
