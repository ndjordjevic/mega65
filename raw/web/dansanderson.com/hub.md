## Landing page — https://dansanderson.com/mega65/

Welcome to my **_Dan’s MEGA65 Digest_**, a monthly newsletter about the [MEGA65 personal computer](https://dansanderson.com/lab-notes/welcome-mega65/).

![Image 1](https://dansanderson.com/mega65/m65colorbanner.png)

The [MEGA65](https://mega65.org/) is a new personal computer based on the [Commodore 65](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Commodore_65), an unreleased prototype of a successor to the venerable [Commodore 64](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Commodore_64). Built with love by Commodore enthusiasts, the MEGA65 is the ultimate modern platform for recreational computing in a retro style.

I'm a member of the MEGA65 Steering Committee, and a regular contributor to the MEGA65 platform, including the [MEGA65 documentation](https://mega65.org/docs), [MEGA65 ROM](https://github.com/MEGA65/mega65-rom-public/blob/main/CHANGELOG.md), and [MEGA65 FPGA core](https://github.com/mega65/mega65-core).

![Image 2](https://dansanderson.com/mega65/bfdemo.png)
