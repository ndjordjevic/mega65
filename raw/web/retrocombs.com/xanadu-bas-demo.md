## Overview

Documents the BAS demo group's Syntax 2022 entry (by Gürçe and Deathy) — only the second MEGA65 demo at a demoparty and the first to showcase VIC-IV capabilities. Captured from real hardware.

## Key Technical Content

- Hardware: real MEGA65 (not emulation)
- Languages: assembly and BASIC
- Graphics focus: VIC-IV (VIC4) capabilities demonstration
- Emulation alternative: Xemu (Windows/Mac/Linux/ChromeOS)
- Event: Syntax 2022, Melbourne, Australia (Nov 11–12)
