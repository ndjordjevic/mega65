## Overview

Unboxing and playthrough of Hibernated 1, an Infocom-style interactive fiction game for the MEGA65 (protagonist wakes from hyper-sleep on a stranded ship).

## Key Technical Content

- Controls: nautical directions (`port`, `starboard`, `fore`, `aft`); shortcuts `p`/`a`/`f`/`s`, `i` inventory, `l` look, `x` examine, `q` quit
- Chain commands with commas (e.g. `starboard,starboard`)
- F1/F3/F5/F7 adjust text/top-bar/border/background colors; saved to `CONFIG.SEQ`
- Loading: mount `.d81`, `MOUNT`, then `BOOT`
- Runs on a Z-Machine interpreter (Infocom-compatible)
