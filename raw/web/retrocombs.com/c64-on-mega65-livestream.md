## Overview

First post in the series exploring the C64 disk image on the MEGA65 onboarding SD card, showcasing demos and games (companion to a 25-min video).

## Key Technical Content

- MEGA65 in C64 mode; onboarding SD card C64 disk image
- Demos: Trap (music), Pikku G Force (scrolling + SID), Natura Morta, C64 Fourier (visual EQ), Subtelevision (physics), PETSCII
- Games: Shadow Switcher (platformer), Spikes (single-screen action)
- SID music integration across demos; USB-C tester used for power verification
